## üí∞ Premium Modules System

### Module Types

#### 1. **Official Modules** (By OpenTrace Team)
- Developed and maintained by the core team
- Available for purchase through official channels
- Include premium support and updates
- Examples: Advanced CRM integrations, AI analytics, Enterprise SSO

#### 2. **Community Modules** (By Third Parties)
- Created by community developers
- Can be free or paid
- Distributed through official marketplace
- Must follow quality and security standards

### Module Licensing

#### For Module Creators
- **Free modules:** Can use any OSI-approved license
- **Paid modules:** Proprietary license allowed
- **Distribution:** Only through official marketplace
- **Attribution:** Must credit original authors
- **Support:** Creators responsible for their modules

#### For Module Users
- **Free modules:** Same license as core platform
- **Paid modules:** Separate commercial license per module
- **Updates:** Handled by module creators
- **Support:** Depends on module type
